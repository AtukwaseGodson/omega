Omega (U) Limited is a construction company that specializes in providing high-quality, affordable engineering solutions in Uganda. We specialize in Civil Engineering, Construction, Consultancy, and Supply of Construction Materials.

About Us

Omega (U) Limited, formed in 2009 and incorporated in November 2010, aims to contribute to the socio-economic development of Uganda by providing high-quality and affordable engineering solutions.

We attained our legal status on November 2nd 2010. Since our inception, We have concentrated on General Construction, Civil Engineering works & Consultancy and general supply of construction materials.

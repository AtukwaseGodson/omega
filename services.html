Our Services

Water & Sanitation

OMEGA (U) Limited is committed to improving access to safe and sustainable water and sanitation solutions for communities. Our comprehensive approach begins with thorough community needs assessments, ensuring that our interventions are tailored to the specific challenges and requirements of each area. We possess extensive expertise in the construction, rehabilitation, and maintenance of a wide range of water sources and sanitation infrastructure.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Gravity Flow Schemes

We specialize in the design, construction, and rehabilitation of gravity flow schemes, leveraging natural topography to deliver clean water to communities without the need for continuous pumping. This includes the development of intake structures, robust transmission pipelines, break-pressure tanks, storage reservoirs, and efficient distribution networks to ensure reliable and cost-effective water supply, particularly in hilly or mountainous regions. Our rehabilitation services focus on restoring functionality, improving efficiency, and extending the lifespan of existing schemes.

Borehole Siting, Drilling, and Rehabilitation

Our services encompass the entire lifecycle of boreholes. We conduct professional hydrological surveys and geophysical investigations for optimal borehole siting, maximizing the chances of successful water abstraction. We then perform expert drilling using modern equipment and techniques, ensuring the installation of durable casings and appropriate pumping mechanisms. Furthermore, we offer comprehensive rehabilitation services for existing boreholes, addressing issues such as siltation, declining yield, pump malfunctions, and structural damage to restore their full operational capacity.

Water Spring Protection

We undertake projects for the protection and enhancement of natural water springs. This involves constructing protective structures around the spring eye to prevent contamination from surface runoff, human activity, and animal intrusion. Our designs prioritize the collection of clean spring water and its safe conveyance to collection points, ensuring a sustainable and safe source of drinking water for communities.

Rainwater Catchment Systems

Recognizing the importance of water harvesting, we design and construct various rainwater catchment systems. These range from household-level roof catchments with storage tanks to larger institutional or community-level systems. Our solutions aim to provide supplementary or primary water sources, especially in areas with seasonal water scarcity, by efficiently collecting, storing, and making rainwater accessible for various uses.

Shallow Wells and Deep Wells

We possess the expertise to construct and rehabilitate both shallow and deep wells, adapting our methods to the local hydrogeological conditions. For shallow wells, we focus on hand-dug or machine-assisted excavation with appropriate lining and protection. For deep wells, we apply advanced drilling techniques to access deeper aquifers. Our rehabilitation services ensure the longevity and functionality of these vital water points.

Construction of Lagoons

OMEGA (U) Limited has experience in the design and construction of various types of lagoons, primarily for wastewater treatment and management. This includes stabilization ponds, aerobic lagoons, and anaerobic lagoons. Our designs aim to provide effective, low-cost, and environmentally friendly solutions for treating domestic and industrial wastewater, promoting safe discharge and potential reuse.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Water Treatment Plants

We provide comprehensive services for water treatment plants, from small-scale community systems to larger municipal facilities. This includes the construction of new plants incorporating processes like coagulation, flocculation, sedimentation, filtration (sand filters, activated carbon filters), and disinfection (chlorination, UV treatment). We also specialize in the rehabilitation of existing plants, upgrading technologies, replacing worn-out components, and optimizing operational efficiency to ensure compliance with water quality standards.

Design and Construction of Urban Water Supply and Distribution Networks

We offer end-to-end solutions for urban water supply and distribution systems. This encompasses the meticulous design of complex networks, including main transmission lines, distribution pipelines, pumping stations, and associated appurtenances. Our construction capabilities ensure the robust and efficient installation of these systems, aimed at delivering reliable, pressurized, and safe drinking water to urban households and businesses, including connections and metering.

Roads and Highway Engineering

OMEGA (U) limited is well equipped to handle highway Engineering projects of the following nature;


Construction and Rehabilitation of Bituminous Asphalt Pavement (BAP)

We specialize in the construction of new and the rehabilitation of existing asphalt pavements. This involves laying down layers of bituminous asphalt concrete, a durable mixture of aggregate (crushed stone, sand, and gravel) bound with bitumen. Our expertise ensures smooth, long-lasting, and resilient road surfaces capable of withstanding various traffic loads and weather conditions common in Uganda. Rehabilitation services focus on restoring structural integrity, improving ride quality, and extending the lifespan of existing asphalt roads.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Bridges and Box Culverts

Bridges and culverts are critical components of any road network. We design and construct robust bridges and box culverts to safely convey traffic over rivers, streams, and other obstacles. Our rehabilitation services for these structures include structural repairs, deck replacement, expansion joint maintenance, and scour protection, ensuring their long-term stability and safety.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Community Access Roads (CARs)

Community Access Roads are vital for connecting rural areas to markets, schools, and healthcare facilities. We undertake projects for the construction and rehabilitation of CARs, often utilizing cost-effective and sustainable methods. Our focus is on improving accessibility for local populations, reducing travel time, and facilitating agricultural and economic activities in remote areas.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Feeder Roads (Surveys and Mapping)

Feeder roads play a crucial role in linking agricultural production areas to main roads. Our services include comprehensive surveys and mapping for new feeder road alignments, as well as condition assessments for existing ones. We then proceed with the construction or rehabilitation, incorporating appropriate drainage systems and surface treatments to ensure their durability and year-round usability, supporting rural livelihoods.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Urban Roads and Highways

In urban centers, efficient road networks are essential for managing traffic flow and supporting economic activity. We design and construct urban roads and highways, focusing on aspects like proper lane demarcation, pedestrian walkways, street lighting, and stormwater drainage. Our rehabilitation work on existing urban infrastructure aims to alleviate congestion, enhance safety, and prolong the service life of these critical routes.

Construction and Rehabilitation of Trenches

While not directly a road surface, trenches are often integral to road projects for utility installation (water pipes, electrical cables, drainage) or for road drainage systems. We offer precise and efficient trenching services, ensuring proper excavation, bedding, pipe-laying, and backfilling, minimizing disruption to the road structure and surrounding areas. Our rehabilitation of trenches ensures stability and prevents future road degradation.
